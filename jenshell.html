Jennifer Shell - Soprano

Jennifer Shell (Soprano) started singing professionally a long time ago! at the age of eight in her native North of England (Wirrall). Under the mentorship of Sir Charles Groves she entered the Royal College of Music where she studied with Redvers Llewllyn. She has had a varied professional solo and choral career, ranging from session work with Sir Elton John and Peter Skellern (amongst others). Appearing on Top of the Pops and singing for over 10 years with the BBC Singers (including First and Last Night of the Proms). Jennifer has sung extensively in recitals and oratorio both in this country and abroad. She has recently retired from a lectureship in music and education at a college of the University of Surrey, and now combines singing commitments with her own Image Consultancy
